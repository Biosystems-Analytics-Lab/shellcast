Terms & Conditions

Tentative Effective Date: March 1st, 2026

1. Introduction

By subscribing to the ShellCast Notification Service, you consent to receiving SMS messages about ShellCast's potential lease closures today, tomorrow, or in 2 days. Message frequency may vary depending on weather conditions, and standard message and data rates may apply.

2. Subscription and Consent

Opt-In process

Text and email notification services are exclusively available to registered users. To register and log in to the ShellCast website, a Gmail address is required. Users must also register their lease location and select a risk category for lease closure at which they will receive notifications: "Moderate or higher," "High or higher," or "Very high."

Opt-in Information

Registered users can manage notification settings via the Preferences link in the top-right corner, by clicking their registered email. To opt-in, check the relevant box, provide a mobile phone number or email address, confirm consent, and save the settings. Users can opt-in to both options.

Opt-In Form Examples

Verification

Upon subscribing, users will receive a one-time verification message. Continued use of the Service confirms your consent.

3. Message Content and Frequency

Types of Messages

Users will be notified about the potential risk of temporary harvest closure for registered leases. The forecasts provide estimates on the risk that a lease site or growing area will temporarily close today, tomorrow, or in 2 days due to excess rainfall in states aside from Florida. Florida utilizes a different excess rainfall system and it is only possible to estimate day-of closure risk. For more information see How ShellCast Works.

Frequency Disclosure

Message frequency depends on the weather conditions. If there's a risk of closure for one of your leases and the closure category matches your notification settings, ShellCast will send a notification between 6:00 a.m. and 7:00 a.m. ET.

4. Opt-Out and Help

Opt-Out Process
Text Notification
  • To stop receiving text messages, reply with any of the following keywords: STOP, QUIT, END, REVOKE, OPT OUT, CANCEL, or UNSUBSCRIBE. Alternatively, visit the Preferences page on the ShellCast website to uncheck the consent box.
Email Notification
  • To stop receiving email notifications, click "Unsubscribe" in any ShellCast notification email or visit the Preferences page on the ShellCast website to uncheck the consent box.
Opt-Out Message Example

"You have successfully opted out of messages from ShellCast forecasting tool. You will receive no further messages."

Help Process

For support or more information, reply HELP to a text notification, visit the FAQs on the ShellCast website, or contact us directly.

Help Message Example

"Thank you for reaching out to ShellCast. For support, please email us at shellcastapp@ncsu.edu. Reply STOP to opt out."
